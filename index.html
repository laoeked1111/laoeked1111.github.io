About Me

Hello! I'm an EECS major at MIT passionate about technologies that lie at the intersection of electronic hardware and signal processing. I love exploring technology through both hardware and software projects, and I am currently doing medical imaging research at Mass General Hospital that tests both my engineering and data processing skills.

Originally from San Antonio, Texas, I came to MIT in the fall of 2024. Prior to college, I attended high school at BASIS San Antonio Shavano. During that time, I participated in various activities like the US Physics Olympiad, National Science Bowl, and the International Science and Engineering Fair. In addition, I was also a member of the Coolidge Scholars Class of 2023.
